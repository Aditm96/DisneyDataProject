Disney Stock Information

Total Disney Stock Price since 2010

The Stock Price has increased over time with sharp increases around 2012, 2016 and 2018. These changes can be attributed to many factors that can impact stock prices, but the biggest factor is the movies and television industry. As Disney bought more properties, the box office's yearly change increased more exponentially.

Disney Stock Price Trendline Since 2010

I used a moving average over 90 days to determine the trend of the daily stock price. The trendline is highlighted in red. I used a moving average to determine the trend because stock price data changes all the time and I needed a method that accounts for that change.
